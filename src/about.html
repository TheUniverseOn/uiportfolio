About Me

Passionate about
design and code

Since I was a child I have always been fascinated by the web. I wanted to create and share with other people, so I start my first online store selling a full print T-shirt and jumpers that I designed. I was hooked by the feedback I was able to get from people that were buying the products. Right there, I recognized the power of feedback, because I could see based on that feedback what sells and what doesn't. I then wanted to make web-related products, So I went to study at Design school, UAL, and graduated from there. I then wanted to make web-related products, So I went to study at Design school, UAL, and graduated from there. I have always been a self-motivated person, so I wanted to understand and get involved directly by creating values for clients. so, I then went to start a small design studio based in London. I manage a small team of freelancers and I am a junior design lead. We've received very positive reviews from clients and were awarded as one of the leading design studios in the UK. Through launching my design studio, I understood the power of communication and untangling complex briefs, requests from clients, business needs, and as well as client management and relationship building. I am currently learning Javascript and React. I want to be a designer who also codes, as I value the power of building projects myself as well as building a strong relationship with engineers.
